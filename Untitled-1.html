About Us

Ekagata Consulting is a consulting firm engaging in accounting, tax, and consulting services registered with the Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Indonesia. We possess expertise in overseeing tax compliance and providing assistance in resolving challenges encountered by taxpayers.

Our Partners are licensed with Certified Indonesia Tax Consultant, Tax Attorney licensed holder, and Chartered Accountant. They are also a member of the Indonesian Tax Consultants Association (IKPI) and Institute of Indonesia Chartered Accountants (IAI). With more than seven years of experience working in a multinational public accounting firm known as Big Four, we deliver a high standard deliverable to our clients.

We provide your company with solutions to minimize your tax burden while ensuring tax compliance and audit assurance. Our services cater to clients across various industries, including manufacturing, trading, construction, digital advertising agencies, and educational foundations.

Our Services

Tax Compliance (Monthly and Annual Tax Return Preparation)

With the growing pace of tax enforcement, compliance to tax regulation is more important than ever. We assist our clients to comply with the prevailing tax regulations by preparing the monthly tax compliance and annual tax compliance. Our tax compliance service covers:

  • Annual Tax Preparation
    • Preparation of Annual Income Tax Returns for Companies
    • Preparation of Annual Income Tax Returns for Individuals
  • Monthly compliance
    • Article 21/26 Withholding Taxes (PPh 21/26)
    • Article 23/26 Withholding Taxes (PPh 23/26)
    • Article 4(2) Withholding Taxes (PPh 4 ayat (2) Final)
    • Article 15 Withholding Taxes (PPh 15)
    • Prepaid Tax Article 25 Instalment (PPh 25)
    • Value Added Tax (PPN)

Accounting Service

Our accounting services cater to clients requiring general accounting and financial planning assistance. Our professionals assist with initial accounting system setup and controls, maintain accounting records, and prepare monthly financial statements.

Assistance in Dealing with Tax Audits

The Indonesia Tax Office (ITO) may perform audits to test taxation obligations, often resulting in significant tax adjustments. We assist you in understanding and defending against these adjustments to minimize tax consequences.

Tax Litigation (Objection, Appeal and Reconsideration Process)

If you disagree with a tax auditor’s adjustments, you may object to the Regional Tax Office, appeal to the Tax Court, and request reconsideration in the Supreme Court. We guide you through the process with proper argument drafting, submission, discussions, and attendance at tribunals.
